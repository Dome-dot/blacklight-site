We are not a fleet. We are a disciplined private military company, bound by precision, loyalty, and results. When governments falter and corporations hesitate—we advance. Discipline before glory. Mission before self. Success before excuses.

Rank Structure

  1. Cadet — entry‑level recruit in training and evaluation.
  2. Ensign — junior officer rank, learning protocol and chain of command.
  3. Junior Operative — transition rank under supervision.
  4. Operative — fully integrated member, cleared for deployments.
  5. Unit Commander — leads squads and directs tactical operations.
  6. Strategic Director — top authority, doctrine and galaxy‑wide oversight.
